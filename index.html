About Hammondsport

ammondsport, New York is a historic village located at the southern tip of Keuka Lake in the Finger Lakes region. It is known for its scenic waterfront, wineries, small-town atmosphere, and strong local community. The area attracts both locals and visitors during the warmer months for boating, dining, and tourism.

Wise Guys Pizza & Grill is part of this local tradition, serving classic American comfort food with a focus on fresh ingredients, fast service, and a welcoming family environment. Whether you are a resident or visiting the lake area, the restaurant is designed to be a casual stop for quality food in a relaxed setting.

Located at 90 Pulteney Street, the restaurant is positioned in the heart of Hammondsport, making it easily accessible from nearby attractions, wineries, and lakefront areas.
